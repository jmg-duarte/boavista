Boavista

The tear-free light theme!

Born out of frustration with existing light themes, which either burn your retinas or have a tutti-frutti assembly of colors.

Boavista strives to be simple and readable — dimming the lights to reduce strain on your eyes and reducing the amount of colors and styles shouting for your attention.

If you want support for your favorite language, or have any suggestions, please file an issue.

This theme was handcrafted by José Duarte. It is available in GitHub under the MIT License.
